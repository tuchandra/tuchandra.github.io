[Paper] Me, My Echo Chamber, and I: Introspection on Social Media Polarization

It’s well-known that “echo chambers” exist online, but to what extent are people aware of them? This paper studies that question by introducing an application to visualize how politically active and polarized users’ Twitter networks are.

2019-12-18 papers, Nabeel Gillani, Ann Yuan, Martin Saveski, Soroush Vosoughi, Deb Roy

[Paper] The spread of true and false news online

This is “that paper on fake news,” with stunningly consistent conclusions. It studies the diffusion of verified true and false news on Twitter, and in particular how the patterns of information spread were different.
